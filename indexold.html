Human Computer Interaction

A course project where our group had to develop a prototype for a potential application. We were given a task to identify a problem, a target group, and a solution. Through an iterative process we disovered that alot of young people found it less amusing to visit musuems. We investigated how we could help these young people to find musuems more attractive. As a result we developed a prototype, Musego. An interactive app to make musueum content more accessible to all visitors.
